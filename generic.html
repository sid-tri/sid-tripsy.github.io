About Me

My name is Siddharth Tripathi.
A Data science professional having 3 years of experience in Retail and Web Domain.
Experience in executing complete life cycle of Data science Projects.
Implemented Deep learning, NLP, recommender systems, BI & data
and feature engineering solutions at production scale.
I am currently looking for jobs in Data Science and Business Intelligence.

I pursued my undergraduate program in Electronics and Communication Engineering at R.V College of Engineering, Bengaluru from August 2012 to May 2016. Opportunities flooded with every coming year, and I got a chance to work on a project which combined IoT with Machine Learning algorithms and was titled as ‘Sewerage Monitoring with Predictive Maintenance using Machine Learning’. Having submitted the research to the International Research Journal of Engineering and Technology (IRJET) for review, we got it published in the IRJET Journal in May 2019. During fourth year, I worked on another project titled ‘Cloud Based Intelligent Traffic System’, which, after rounds of review, was published in 3rd International Conference on Trends in Electronics and Informatics (ICOEI), sponsored by IEEE. During the four years of undergrad studies, I learned software programming skills by taking electives like OOPS with C++ and Data Structures. These skills helped me during the campus placement and I was able to secure a job at one of the largest American Bank namely “Wells Fargo”. I joined Wells Fargo as an Analyst and worked under the Consumer Lending Technology department as a part of the Home Equity Business Intelligence Team. My team was responsible for driving ‘Technology Health Refresh (THR)’ activity which involved upgrading platform architecture for the online banking application, wherein I contributed by developing, optimizing and maintaining data architectures.

In Sep 2017, I joined Rankethon, an education-tech services company, as a Senior Associate. I was responsible for planning, forecasting and developing deep business insight for the third-party syndication and distribution network of the educational institutions throughout the country. I designed, developed and established KPIs to monitor analysis and provide strategic insights to drive performance in all delivery measures including Marketing and Customer Service metrics using Tableau and SAS. I got an opportunity to expand my knowledge to implement Machine Learning & Deep Learning algorithms using Python and R in solving complex analytics problems. My enthusiasm, coupled with the scope for innovations and opportunities available in the field of Data Analytics, has kept me motivated to contribute to the industry.
